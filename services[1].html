Services

Comprehensive occupational health testing to help you meet regulatory requirements.

Audiometric Testing

On-site hearing assessment and surveillance to detect early signs of occupational noise-induced hearing loss. Our testing and reporting are designed to help employers comply with relevant sections of the Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) Act concerning hearing conservation and monitoring.

Mask Fit Testing

Qualitative and quantitative respirator fit-testing to ensure that tight-fitting masks and respirators provide the intended protection. Fit-testing is a critical component of a respiratory protection program and supports compliance with OHS Act requirements for worker respiratory safety.

Hearing Protection Devices (HPD)

HPD selection, fit-checks, and user training to ensure hearing protectors are effective in your workplace. We provide guidance and documentation to support your hearing conservation program under the OHS Act.
